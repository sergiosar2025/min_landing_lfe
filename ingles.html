Regarding LFE:

Lenguage French and English is a language school dedicated to teaching English and French to all ages. Our commitment is to provide an engaging, efficient, and enjoyable learning experience that adapts to the needs and pace of each student. With a team of qualified teachers and modern methodologies, we combine theory and practice to develop the four essential skills: speaking, listening, reading, and writing. Our classes are dynamic and interactive, promoting real communication from the very first moments. At Lenguage French and English, we believe that learning a new language opens doors to the world—whether for travel, study, work, or connecting with new cultures. Here, each student is encouraged to reach their full potential and confidently conquer new horizons.
